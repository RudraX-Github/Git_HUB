This file describes the columns in the Agmarknet_Price_Report.csv file.


State: Full name of the Indian state where the market is located. Example: Gujarat
District: District within the state where the market operates. Example: Surat
Market: Specific market or mandi name. Example: Bardoli
Commodity: Name of the agricultural product being traded. Example: Cabbage
Variety: Specific variety or type of the commodity. Example: Other
Grade: Quality classification of the commodity (e.g., FAQ = Fair Average Quality). Example: FAQ
Arrival_Date: Date when the commodity arrived at the market (format: DD-MM-YYYY). Example: 05-12-2015
Min_Price: Minimum price recorded for the commodity on that day. Example: 0
Max_Price: Maximum price recorded for the commodity on that day. Example: 2005
Modal_Price: Most frequently quoted price (modal price). Example: 2005
Commodity_Code: System-generated code representing the commodity. Example: 154
